Case Studies

Selected product initiatives showing how I approached discovery, execution, and measurable impact in sportsbook environments.

Player Props Discovery & Frontend Navigation

Player props were mainly accessible from the match page, which limited visibility and reduced engagement with high-interest basketball player markets.

I introduced dedicated navigation for Basketball Players markets and improved the discovery flow so relevant markets were easier to reach from the main sportsbook experience. The work focused on frontend structure, visibility, and market placement rather than burying props inside deeper match views.

Problem

Low discoverability of player props.

Action

Dedicated frontend navigation and improved UX structure.

Result

Approx. 20% uplift in player props play rate.

Proof Artifact

701% Parlay Bonus — CMS Visibility & Risk Controls

Promotional mechanics needed to be commercially strong, visually clear on frontend, and safe from abuse.

I worked on the product logic behind the 701% Parlay Bonus, including CMS-driven odds highlighting, bonus visibility, and abuse-prevention mechanics. The objective was to combine strong promotional impact with manageable execution across different campaign states and operational needs.

Problem

Complex promo flow with risk of user confusion and misuse.

Action

Defined CMS-driven frontend logic and fraud-prevention rules.

Result

Stronger promo visibility and more controlled execution.

Proof Artifact

Multi-Market CMS Customization for White-Label Delivery

A single static sportsbook setup could not support the commercial, language, and presentation needs of diverse European and African markets.

I helped architect CMS capabilities for white-label customization of homepage content, offers, and market-specific presentation. This enabled localized delivery with less engineering dependency and supported a more scalable operating model across operator-like markets.

Problem

Different markets required different UX, content, and pricing emphasis.

Action

Built CMS-based white-label customization across 9 markets.

Result

Faster rollout execution and stronger local market fit.

Proof Artifact

How I Work

My approach combines sportsbook domain expertise, structured product delivery, and close collaboration with technical and operational teams.
